    If you are interested in using the common reading book Tales of Two Americas: Stories of Inequality in a Divided Nation edited by John Freeman for use in a course or for campus programming, and would like a digital exam copy, please fill in the form below. That is, our aim is to support student use of the book by making it easier for our faculty to incorporate the book into their work with our WSU students.

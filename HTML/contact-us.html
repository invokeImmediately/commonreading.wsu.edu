Since it began in 2006-07, the Common Reading Program has introduced thousands of WSU students to shared academic dialogues around a number of carefully selected books. Students (current, past, and prospective), families, community members, educators, and others can find a wealth of information on this website about the program. But sometimes, it’s most appropriate to get in touch with an expert on the Common Reading who can answer questions and provide insights.

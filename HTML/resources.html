1. Common Reading

Resources for Working with Tales of Two Americas

For faculty using Tales of Two Americas in first-year and other courses and programming throughout the 2021-22 academic year, there will be a wide array of resources available to supplement or augment your teaching.

Be sure to also check the Common Reading calendar page for the latest updates on scheduled speakers and events offered to students outside of classes. Any questions or suggestions should be directed to Karen Weathermon, director of the Common Reading Program, at kweathermon@wsu.edu.
